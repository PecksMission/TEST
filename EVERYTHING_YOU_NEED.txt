================================================================================
USER FLOW
================================================================================

1. New User Lands on Homepage
   ↓ Sees featured journeys
   ↓ Clicks "Register"

2. Registration
   ↓ Fills form (email, username, password)
   ↓ Clicks Register
   ↓ Automatically logged in
   ↓ Redirected to Journey Builder

3. Create Journey
   ↓ Opens chat interface
   ↓ Types "I had Chiari surgery..."
   ↓ Claude responds with questions
   ↓ Continues conversation
   ↓ AI generates structure
   ↓ Preview updates in real-time
   ↓ Clicks "Publish"

4. Published Journey
   ↓ Beautiful timeline displays
   ↓ Gets shareable link
   ↓ Can share with others
   ↓ Others can comment and react
   ↓ Appears in dashboard

5. View Dashboard
   ↓ Can see all journeys
   ↓ Can edit or delete
   ↓ Can publish new journeys
   ↓ Can view statistics
