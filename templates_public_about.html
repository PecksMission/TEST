About Surgery Day Builder

Our Mission

Surgery Day Builder was created to help people share their healthcare stories. We believe that health journeys are deeply personal—combining clinical facts with faith, family support, and personal resilience.

Too often, health information is fragmented across different platforms and people. We created a space where you can organize your complete story in one beautiful, shareable timeline.

How It Works

Using advanced AI, Surgery Day Builder helps you create a professional health journey timeline through natural conversation. You describe your experience, and Claude AI helps structure it into stages, timeline entries, and visual elements.

The Three Dimensions of Your Story

Clinical

Medical facts, procedures, test results, and professional care

Faith

Spiritual moments, prayers, and personal meaning

Family

Support from loved ones and community

Why Surgery Day Builder?

  • Easy to Create — Chat with AI, not forms
  • Beautiful to Share — Professional timelines anyone can read
  • Complete Story — Clinical + spiritual + personal
  • Community Support — Comments and reactions from others
  • Private or Public — You control who sees your journey

Built on Proven Values

Surgery Day Builder is inspired by the medical philosophy of William Osler: "It is much more important to know what sort of a patient has a disease than what sort of a disease a patient has."

We believe healthcare storytelling matters. Your journey—from diagnosis to recovery—deserves to be heard and celebrated.
